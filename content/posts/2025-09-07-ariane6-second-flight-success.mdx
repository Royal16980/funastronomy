Europe's **Ariane 6** completed a flawless second mission, placing multiple payloads into their intended orbits. The performance strengthens Europe's independent access to space and relieves pressure on rideshare bottlenecks.

### Flight highlights
- Target orbits met within tolerances.
- Upper stage performed to plan, enabling a secondary deployment.
- Teams cited improved turnaround time on the pad.
